Our History

The Journey of Aurelia Pizza

From a humble family kitchen to a beloved name in every neighborhood — Aurelia Pizza’s story is one of passion, tradition, and dedication.

1998

The Beginning

It all started in a small home kitchen where the Aurelia family perfected their secret dough recipe — crisp, golden, and irresistibly soft inside.

2005

Our First Store

With local support and word-of-mouth love, the first Aurelia Pizza store opened, introducing authentic wood-fired pizzas to the community.

2015

Growing the Family

New branches blossomed across cities, but our promise stayed the same: handcrafted pizzas, fresh ingredients, and warm smiles in every slice.

2024

Today & Beyond

Aurelia Pizza continues to blend innovation with tradition — bringing our signature flavors to new homes, new hearts, and new generations.
