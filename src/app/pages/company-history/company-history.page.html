Our History

The Journey of Primo Pizza

Before the first store, before the sign was ever hung, there was only a small kitchen and a family that loved to cook. What started as quiet evenings filled with laughter and the smell of rising dough slowly grew into a tradition. Over the years, that tradition would carry the name Primo Pizza.

1998

The Beginning

In 1998, inside a humble home kitchen, the Primo family began shaping what would become their legacy. Night after night they experimented with flour, yeast, and patience until the dough came out just right; crisp on the outside, soft within. The recipe was simple, but it carried a warmth that made everyone who tried it feel at home.

2005

Our First Store

After years of encouragement from friends and neighbors, the family decided to share their creation with the world. In 2005, the first Primo Pizza store opened its doors. The scent of wood-fired dough and fresh basil drifted through the air as locals gathered inside. There were no fancy signs or grand celebrations, only the promise of honest food and a welcoming table. By evening, every seat was taken, and the dream that once lived in a small kitchen had finally taken shape.

2015

Growing the Family

Ten years later, Primo Pizza had become more than a neighborhood favorite. New branches opened across nearby cities, each one guided by the same care and heart as the original. From bustling diners to quiet family meals, people found a sense of familiarity in every slice. The brand grew, but its soul remained in the shared smiles and the aroma that filled each store.

2024

Today & Beyond

Today, Primo Pizza stands as a reflection of where it all began. The recipes have evolved, the ovens have changed, and the world around us keeps moving, yet our purpose has stayed the same. Every pizza that leaves our oven carries a piece of the family story that started it all. And as we look toward the future, we carry the same promise we made on our first day: to make food with heart and serve it with pride.
